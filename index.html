Porsche

The Porsche 911 (sometimes called the Porsche 911 Carrera) is a sports car produced by Porsche AG of Stuttgart, Germany, since 1964. The model has undergone continuous development since its introduction at the 1963 Frankfurt Motor Show under the name 901. Mechanically, it is characterized by a rear engine and, until the 993 model in 1998, air cooling.
