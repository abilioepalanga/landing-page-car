Porsche

The Porsche 911 (sometimes called the Porsche 911 Carrera) is a sports car produced by Porsche AG of Stuttgart, Germany, since 1964. The model has undergone continuous development since its introduction at the 1963 Frankfurt Motor Show under the name 901. Mechanically, it is characterized by a rear engine and, until the 993 model in 1998, air cooling.

Ferrari

Ferrari is an Italian luxury sports car manufacturer based in Maranello. Founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1939 in the racing division of Alfa Romeo under the name Auto Avio Costruzioni, the company built its first car in 1940. However, the company's beginnings as a car manufacturer are generally recognized as 1947, when the first car bearing the Ferrari name was completed.

Lamborghini

Lamborghini is an Italian manufacturer of luxury, high-performance sports cars originally created to compete with Ferrari, based in Sant'Agata Bolognese. The company, which was founded in 1963 by Ferruccio Lamborghini (1916-1993) as a subsidiary of his successful tractor factory Lamborghini Trattori S.p.A., is now owned by the Volkswagen AG group as a subsidiary of the Audi AG division.
